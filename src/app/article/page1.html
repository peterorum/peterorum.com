Having been a software developer for many years, I am particularly interested in importing that knowledge and process into the traditional medium of painting, rather than simply staying with my computer skills and using new digital mediums. This essay focuses on those particular works, as they interested me most. There was a development in my work over the course from first just copying digital images, then creating paintings using programming methods, through to paintings about the social impact of computers, networks and databases and their impact on privacy.

This document was produced as part of the Master of Art degree at the College of Fine Arts, University of New South Wales.
